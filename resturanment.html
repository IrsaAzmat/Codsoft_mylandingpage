About Us

Why Choose us?

At Foodie , we’re serving up more than one type of food. In fact, Foodie Famous Chines cusine is one of our unexpected specialties. Reminiscent of butcher shops back in the day, each slow-smoked, sizzling prime chop measures seven-fingers high. Our signature recipe, that we have perfected for more than four decades, is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings, cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it’s topped with Foodie signature herb-garlic butter, then carved tableside.
